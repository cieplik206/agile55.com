The X Factor

In a nutshell

Our team have architected, developed and designed a fully-functional game for The X Factor brand called Fame Game.

  • Using Agile Approach and weekly meetings we were able to implement all of the complex logic of an Web-RPG game within 6 week timeline.
  • Reacting to our client feedback we have made a very critical change in the middle of the development by severely improving gameplay, look and logic of the game.
  • A whole web game was implemented as AJAX application running on Agile Toolkit and controlled through a flexible administration system.
  • The whole project have received a custom look and feel making it follow The X Factor guidelines perfectly.
  • The game was fully integrated with Facebook allowing you to compete against your friends.
