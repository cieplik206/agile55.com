In a nutshell

Envisioned by the same time who have launched zopa.co.uk, LinkedFinance.com has contacted Agile team in search of capable, fast and professional software provider. Initially our client was planning to eventually move development in-house but after a very successful collaboration have decided to continue using Agile services.

We have worked with AppsFuel to architect the initial concept of their software on top of Agile Toolkit.

  • Software we have created using Agile Toolkit literally controls cash value of over a million euros.
  • Roughly every week LinkedFinance enables one new business to receive direct loan from hundreds of peer-to-peer investors.
  • Throughout development of the project, our team have took security precautions and communicated with third party security reviewers to make sure our product is rock-solid.
  • We have helped LinkedFinance grow past a single instance and it's currently running in an Amazon Cloud infrastructure.
