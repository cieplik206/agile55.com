Skills

skills

Below are skills for ideal applicant. If you are lacking some of those - that's fine - we have training program for our new employees where you will be able to catch up with the rest of us.

Primary skills we are looking for in our applicatns are:

  • self-motivation;
  • precision;
  • creativity;
  • efficiency.
We are interested in hiring people in age 22-30 who are very passionate about their development.

Ideal applicant should have a bachelors degree in computer science and should be familiar with algorythm efficiency and optimization to some degree. You must understand how to write code efficiency, be very well familiar with object oriented programming practices and use them fluently. 2 year experience in PHP is required, but you should know how to develop in some other object-oriented language such as Java, Pascal or C++. You should also be familiar with abstraction and development of universal code and routines. You must be adaptive in terms of coding style

Applicant should be comfortable with working in Linux command-line environment, vim, using Subversion or Git. You should also be able to install MAMP, LAMP or WAMP on your local computer and develop localy. Code you develop should be properly commented and very efficient in terms of lines-of-code, CPU and Memory. You must know and use all the available resources of underlying language. You are encouraged to introduce improvements and enhancements, create reusable addons and modules and also providing documentation for them.

You must feel comfortable with your skills. When presented with a task you should be able to estimate how much time it would take for you to develop. You must be able to work in a team, collaborate, put suggestions and use on-line collaboration tools - email, jabber, voice confernce, screen sharing.

It is rather important that you could touch-type and be able to type at least 40 words per minute in English. When it comes to jabber conversation â€“ if you are struggling to find the right keys - it's slowing down work of your team-mates.

English understanding is most essential. Often you would need to understand feedback provided by our clients, which sometimes is not very straightforward. We would be glad to offer you help if you have questions, but you should be able to figure things out on your own.

Finally - we are offering you a full-time position. Which means that we expect you to work solely for Agile. We do not expect you to overwork, merely be productive and effective. If you do not have immediate tasks then it's your job to find out what you can work on next. If no task is available - work on self-improvement. You will need to build a habit of tracking time you spend. We are not too picky about your time, but we need to know which project you have worked on. This is important for budget allocation and our internal accounting and project management.

Evaluation Test

evaluation

While we do review your Resume, CV or recommendation letters, we prefer to give our applicatns a real-life test. If you are willing to try yourself out - fill out the form on the right.

We recommend that before taking evaluation test - get to know our company better. Look at the software we produced, certainly look at demos, download, watch screencasts and play with Agile Toolkit. All our projects are using Agile Toolkit, so make sure it does not go against your ideals.

When we receive your form details we will setup a evaluation test for you and will email you instructions. During evaluation test, you will be provided with access to a temporary SVN repository. You should follow instructions to download and install web software on your local computer. Next 2 hours you are expected to work on the test-project and commit your results back. Not only your development abilities will be tested, but also your ability to suggest improvements, understanding of usability, listening to requirements and so on. During the test you can choose the path you are most comfortable with - either fix bugs or improve or enhance. Everything counts.

Based on your test result we can discuss terms of your develpment position. We might be able to hire you either full-time on per-project basis while you finish with your existing projects, studies or employments.

Remember - we are flexible. Shoud you have any questions - please contact us. Now - let's change the web together!
