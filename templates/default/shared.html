web and mobile software company.

Elexu Technologies is a creative software development and consultancy company based in the heart of Soho, London and is a wholly-owned subsidiary within the Elexu Portfolio group of companies. Our specialisation is in interactive Mobile-to-TV applications, from interactive games through to internet TV formats which engage their audiences via real-time interactions to produce a more realistic, responsive and entertaining means of communication between content producers and content viewers.

Services

Web Development

What differentiates our development services from other high calibre development houses is the exceptional scalability and robustness of our technologies. You can be confident that whatever we design and build for you will be built with longevity and consistent performance in mind.

App Development

We cater for a wide range of App development for both Mobile and Tablet including iPhone and Android, utilising HTML5 as a universal language that works across all the platforms. Our area of expertise lies in the complex field of Mobile-to-TV interactive applications for Internet Protocol Television (IPTV).

Web Design

A creative team of designers add the polish and chrome to our structured code to present the most up-to-date and cutting edge look and feel to whatever platform, application or programme we construct. Our designers are passionate about their craft and will work closely with you to ensure the design meets your requirements.

Consultancy

Many corporations and content providers have ambitious projects which require a high degree of expert advice and specification. One of our key assets for these clients is our consultancy service for maintaining and optimizing network, security, server and storage infrastructures.

Support

Our support services are tailored to each client's needs. It is in our best interests to ensure your software’s performance is maintained but is also supported 24/7 by experts on hand, and not outsourced. By providing a quality support service we help our business partners maintain high levels of user satisfaction.

Approach

Consultation

Elexu Technologies takes a highly consultative approach to working with clients. This investment in our clients pays huge dividends for all parties involved. Through many years of delivering successful web, mobile and apps development projects, we know this approach consistently produces outstanding results for both our clients and ourselves.

 

Project Management

We excel at providing a robust project management process to ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget. In addition to using Agile methodologies to drive the development stream (sprints, scrums and iterative releases), we also employ leading task management and communication software to ensure all resources are coordinated in their activities, deliverables and timelines.

Communication

Clients of Elexu Technologies benefit from having a dedicated relationship manager. We pride ourselves on timely and transparent communication and aim to build enduring partnerships with all our clients as opposed to a transactional based relationships. We fundamentally believe a partnership approach is the foundation of our success.

 

Support

Our relationship with clients doesn’t end simply with the delivery of software. We invest in aftercare so our clients can rest assured that their projects are always up and running (99.9% availability). For mission critical applications we can provide our partners with 24/7 expert support or support on a pay per task basis.
