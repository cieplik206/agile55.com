Who we are?

Our team was establised in 1998 and implemented our first web framework in '99. We were early advocates of "extreme programming" and later "agile methodologies". Our biggest achievement is release of Agile Toolkit as a separate product / business. It is now the most popular PHP UI Framework.

Our team consists of a dozen 10+ year industry experts located in multiple geographical locations - UK, Ireland and Eastern Europe. We challenge ourselves to improve our efficiency while enjoying the work on some of the most sophisticated Web Start-up projects.

 

Our Services

We offer bespoke web software development for SMEs and Star-ups. Every day we prove that a small, agile and talented team could out-perform a large, office-based, 9-to-5 team.

We are very particular about our technologies. We work with non-technical client who trusted us to do best application architecture design, right technology choices and most suitable deployment strategy.

In addition we train in-house teams, document, deploy, maintain, support (SLA) and advise on your projects throughout their life-cycle.
